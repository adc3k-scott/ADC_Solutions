## The gate (everything is contingent on this)

**Interconnection.** The plan's moat and biggest diligence item: an
independent Entergy large-load / MISO inquiry for the site, yielding a
written, quantified servable-MW figure — filed by ADC, not WGT, before
first contact. The earlier "450 MW already wired / nothing to confirm"
claim must be reframed as "interconnect head start, MW subject to
Entergy/MISO study." Interconnection confirmation is a condition
precedent in the LOI. [W] — plan §Phase 0, §Phase 3, §What makes or
breaks it.

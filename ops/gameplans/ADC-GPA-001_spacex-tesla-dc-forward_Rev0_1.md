## §3 The new hook — AI data centers in space

Musk stated at IPO that proceeds fund 100,000+ satellites and AI data
centers in space (SPACEX-RESEARCH-002 §A). ADC's read: orbital compute
multiplies the terrestrial ground segment, the hard problem is thermal
(liquid loops to radiators — CDU/TCS-class engineering), and the
terrestrial Colossus-pattern buildout (gas + grid batteries) is the
node product's proven analog. Pitch Rev 0.3 carries one truthful
paragraph on this; no topology claim changes.

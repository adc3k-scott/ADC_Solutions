## §1 Thesis

ADC controls — and on this plan comes to own — **three brownfield
properties in Lafayette, Louisiana for under ~$10M total**, against a
documented greenfield replacement cost of $19.4M–$26.6M for the
Evangeline building alone (build-cost comparison memo, 2026-05-30).
Two sit on the Evangeline Thruway corridor; the third is the 22-acre
Trappey's cannery on the Vermilion River. All three are heavy
industrial structures with strong, thick foundations — they need
updating and securing, not rebuilding [W — Scott 2026-06-11; structural
assessments are diligence gates, §2].

The properties serve both business lines (MISSION.md): manufacturing
and distribution of the modular product line (BL1), and the compute
sites and Mission Control head-end (BL2). The seed raise funds
property control, the Marlie Hub validation POC
([ADC-MHUB-001](../../colo/sites/marlie-hub-engineering/ADC-MHUB-001_Rev0_2.md) —
referenced, not restated), manufacturing start-up, and a
deadline-driven solar element (§6).

## C. Long bio (≈180 words — for a dedicated bio page)

Scott Tomsu is the founder and president of ADC Solutions, Louisiana's
AI-native builder of mission-critical compute infrastructure. His through-line is
simple and rare: he doesn't just operate complex systems — he designs the hardest
part of the system and ships it.

For 25 years that meant the deepwater Gulf and West Africa, where as an IMCA ROV
Superintendent and certified commercial diver he ran subsea robotics to 3,000
meters — managing the high-voltage power, fiber-optic signal, and uptime of
machines working at the end of a long, fragile link. On land, he is a licensed
Louisiana general contractor (active since 2003) who has self-performed homes, a
medical center, and commercial work across Acadiana. He is also a championship
motorcycle drag racer — 2023 AMRA Racer of the Year — who designs and builds his
own race engines.

In early 2026 he turned the whole stack toward AI: he deployed a live LLM voice
agent, then built ADC Solutions and runs it through autonomous AI. Power, fiber,
thermal, structure, and now models — one discipline, applied end to end.

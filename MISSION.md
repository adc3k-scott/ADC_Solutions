# ADC Solutions Inc — Mission

ADC Solutions Inc is Louisiana's first AI-native business model, positioned to
scale for the next generation of bottlenecks in mission-critical,
high-performance AI compute.

## Business line 1: Modular infrastructure manufacturing & distribution

From our facilities in Lafayette, Louisiana, we assemble modular infrastructure
components:

- **Cooling Distribution Units (CDUs)** — 2 MW liquid-to-liquid heat exchange
  units, with ultra-high-density 5 MW modules actively in development.
- **Modular Technology Cooling Systems (TCS)** — offsite clean-room assembly
  ensures the exceptional fluid cleanliness required for high-density chip
  cooling while accelerating on-site construction through plug-and-play
  installation.
- **Hot Aisle Containment (HAC) Structures** — custom-built architectural
  frameworks with immediate drop-in compatibility with the Google / OCP
  Redmond bay (28" × 4-wide, fully engineered); an NVIDIA MGX (600 mm)
  bay variant is in development pending an NVIDIA/OEM site-planning data
  package (see `ops/research/2026-06-10-nvidia-mgx-hac-variant.md`).
- **10 MW Natural Gas Genset Microgrid** — a fungible 10 MW natural gas genset
  microgrid designed to scale, fully controlled through our unified Redfish
  telemetry architecture.
- **Telemetry** — our AI-native operating system: the platform layer that
  operates the four hardware products as one machine — one Redfish
  namespace, one clock, a defined autonomy ladder, and hard Layer-1 safety
  bounds (see `products/Telemetry/`).

ADC Solutions is positioning itself as a large-scale distributor of these
integrated products, leveraging Lafayette's deep supplier base in oilfield
fluid-handling disciplines and proximity to major logistics hubs: the Port of
Iberia for worldwide shipping, railway access, and the I-49 / I-10 split.
Our current production run includes units for our colo development sites and
demo models, with the goal of evolving from a regional infrastructure provider
into a Made-in-the-USA leader.

## Business line 2: Louisiana Colocation Solutions Provider (CSP)

Leveraging our in-house Louisiana General Contractor License, we are focused on:

1. **Brownfield repurposing** — older commercial/industrial locations that
   previously consumed large amounts of electricity, with on-prem natural gas,
   that can accommodate 5–10 MW IT compute clusters (NVIDIA GPUs or Google
   TPUs). These sites are ideal because they already have transmission,
   substations, switching infrastructure, industrial zoning, natural gas
   piping, and typically local support for continued industrial use.
2. **AI Enterprise for oilfield services** — introduce AI enterprise compute
   and its resale value to Louisiana oilfield service companies.
3. **Greenfield development** — projects with large amounts of on-prem natural
   gas and water.

## Forward outlook

By capitalizing on our deep expertise in robotics, aviation, drone technology,
and electronics, we plan to lead the adoption of these innovations as they
become available.
